# Sample Preparation & Separations

Welcome to what is, honestly, the most unglamorous and most consequential part of any top-down experiment. If your proteoforms don't survive the trip from biology to the ESI tip in a recognizable shape, nothing downstream — no fancy fragmentation, no clever deconvolution, no elegant scoring — can save you. The good news is that the community has learned a tremendous amount over the last decade about how to keep intact proteins happy, and the workflows below are now genuinely approachable. Let's walk through them together.
